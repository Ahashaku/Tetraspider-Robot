Tetraspider - Quadruped Walking Robot

Introduction

Tetraspider is a four-legged quadruped robot designed for rugged terrains and space exploration simulations. It demonstrates advanced servo coordination, wireless control, and real-time maneuverability.

Key Features

  • Four-legged motion with 12 SG90 servos
  • Pick and place mechanism using MG996 servo
  • Wireless control via Bluetooth and mobile app
  • Optimized power management with 4600mAh battery
